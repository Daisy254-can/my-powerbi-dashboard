# 🛒 Retail Store Price Analysis Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard comparing product prices across three major retail stores: **Walmart**, **Target**, and **Costco**.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard contains **2 visualizations**:

### 1. Total by Store
- Compares the **total sum of prices** across all three stores
- **Walmart** leads with **225.15**
- **Target** is second at **219.07**
- **Costco** is third at **210.02**

### 2. Best Store for Product
- Breaks down pricing **by product category** across all three stores
- Products tracked:
  - **Rice**
  - **Dried Beans**
  - **Bottled Water**
  - **Canned Vegetables**
- Helps identify which store offers the best price per product

---
